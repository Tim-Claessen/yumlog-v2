# Mars Bar Slice

## Ingredients

- 180g of Mars Bars, chopped
- 90g unsalted butter
- 3 cups Rice Bubbles (puffed rice cereal)
- 200g milk chocolate, chopped or chips

## Instructions

1. **Prepare a slice tin** (approximately 18x28cm) by greasing and lining with baking paper.
2. **Melt Mars Bars** and butter together in a saucepan over low heat, stirring continuously until smooth. Add golden syrup if using.
3. Remove from heat and **quickly stir** in the Rice Bubbles until well coated.
4. **Press the mixture** evenly into the prepared tin using the back of a spoon or spatula.
5. **Melt the chocolate** gently in a microwave or over a double boiler until smooth.
6. **Pour** the melted chocolate over the slice and spread evenly.
7. **Refrigerate** for at least 1 hour or until set.
8. **Slice** into squares or bars using a warm knife for clean cuts. Store in an airtight container in the fridge.
